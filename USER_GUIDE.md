## Quick Start (5 Minutes)

### Step 1: Open the Application
1. Find the file named `index.html` in your folder
2. Double-click it
3. It will open in your web browser

### Step 2: Start Creating a Contract
1. Click the big purple button that says **"Start New Contract"**
2. You'll see the first question appear

### Step 3: Fill in the Information
1. Answer each question one at a time
2. You'll see examples in gray text showing you what to enter
3. Click **"Next"** to move to the next question
4. Click **"Previous"** if you need to go back

**Progress Bar:** The blue bar at the top shows how far along you are!

### Step 4: Review Your Answers
1. After the last question, click **"Review"**
2. You'll see all your answers on one screen
3. Check everything carefully
4. If something is wrong, click **"Edit Information"** to fix it

### Step 5: Generate the PDF
1. When everything looks good, click **"Generate PDF Contract"**
2. Wait a few seconds while the PDF is created
3. The PDF will automatically download to your computer
4. Look in your Downloads folder for the file

### Step 6: Done!
- Your contract is ready to use!
- You can create another contract by clicking **"Create Another Contract"**

## What Information Do I Need?

Before you start, have this information ready:

### About the Lease
- When does the lease start? (Example: 9 January 2026)
- When does the lease end? (Example: 11 May 2026)
- How many nights? (Example: 123)
- What date should appear on the contract? (Example: 18 November 2025)

### About the Tenant
- Full name or organization name (Example: University of Notre Dame)
- Street address (Example: 11 Walsh Family Hall of Architecture)
- City, State, ZIP (Example: Notre Dame, IN 46556)
- Phone number (Example: +1 (574) 631-5000)
- Email address (Example: contact@nd.edu)

### About the Properties
- **Property 1:**
  - Full address (Example: Via Dandolo 10, Scale A, Int. 2, 00153 Rome, Italy)
  - Details (Example: 2 STUDENTS (2 Bedrooms, 1 Bathroom))

- **Property 2 (if you have a second property):**
  - Full address
  - Details
  - *Leave blank if you only have one property*

### About the Rental
- How many students total? (Example: 5)
- How many apartments total? (Example: 2)
- Rent per night per student in Euros (Example: 50.00)
- Security deposit in Euros (Example: 1000.00)
- Pet cleaning fee in Euros (Example: 200.00)

## Common Questions

### Q: Can I save my progress and come back later?
**A:** No, the system doesn't save your progress. You need to complete the contract in one session. It only takes about 5 minutes!

### Q: Can I edit the contract after generating the PDF?
**A:** No, once the PDF is generated, you can't edit it. You would need to create a new contract with the correct information.

### Q: What if I make a mistake?
**A:** Before generating the PDF, you can click "Previous" to go back and fix any field. After reviewing, you can click "Edit Information" to make changes.

### Q: Where does the PDF get saved?
**A:** The PDF downloads to your computer's Downloads folder. The file name will be something like `RomaRentals_Contract_2025-11-21.pdf`.

### Q: Can I use this on my phone or tablet?
**A:** Yes! The system works on phones, tablets, and computers.

### Q: Do I need internet to use this?
**A:** You need internet the first time to load the PDF library. After that, it works offline.

### Q: Is my data safe?
**A:** Yes! All your data stays in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server. Once you close the browser, the data is gone.

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: The PDF didn't download
**Solution:**
1. Check your Downloads folder - it might be there
2. Check if your browser blocked the download (look for a message at the top)
3. Try clicking "Download Again"
4. Try a different browser (Chrome works best)

### Problem: I can't click "Next"
**Solution:**
- Make sure you filled in the field (required fields have a * next to them)
- Check that you entered the information in the correct format

### Problem: The page looks weird
**Solution:**
- Try refreshing the page (but you'll lose your data)
- Try zooming to 100% (press Ctrl+0 on Windows or Cmd+0 on Mac)
- Try a different browser

### Problem: I accidentally closed the browser
**Solution:**
- Unfortunately, you'll need to start over
- The system doesn't save your progress
- Next time, keep the browser open until you download the PDF
