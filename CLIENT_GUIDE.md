# RomaRentals Contract Generator - Client Guide

## What Is This?

This is a **web-based contract generator** that makes filling out your rental contracts super easy. Instead of manually editing a Word document or PDF, you just answer simple questions, and the system creates a perfect, professional contract for you.

## How Does It Work?

Think of it like filling out a form online, but instead of getting a receipt, you get a complete legal contract!

### The Process:

1. **Open the webpage** (just double-click `index.html`)
2. **Pick which contract** you want to create
3. **Answer questions** one at a time (like "What's the tenant's name?")
4. **Review your answers** to make sure everything is correct
5. **Generate the contract** - it creates a perfect PDF-style document
6. **Print or save** it as a PDF

## What Information Do You Need?

For the **Rental Agreement**, you'll need:

- Effective date of the lease
- Tenant's full name
- Tenant's address, city, state, zip
- Tenant's phone and email
- Property address(es)
- Number of students and bedrooms
- Lease start and end dates
- Number of nights
- Rent amount per night
- Security deposit amount
- Pet cleaning fee
- Today's date for signature

**Tip:** Have all this information ready before you start, and it will take you less than 5 minutes to create a complete contract!

## Saving Your Contracts

### To Save as PDF:

1. After generating the contract, click "Print Contract"
2. In the print dialog, choose "Save as PDF" or "Microsoft Print to PDF"
3. **IMPORTANT:** Turn OFF "Headers and footers" (see note below)
4. Choose where to save it
5. Done!

### To Print:

1. Click "Print Contract"
2. Select your printer
3. **IMPORTANT:** Turn OFF "Headers and footers" (see note below)
4. Click Print
5. Done!

## Common Questions

### Q: Do I need internet to use this?
**A:** No! Once you have the files, it works completely offline.

### Q: Can I edit the contract after generating it?
**A:** You can go back and change your answers, then generate a new contract. The generated contract itself is meant to be final.

### Q: What if I make a mistake?
**A:** Just click "Back" to go to previous questions, or start over and fill it out again. It only takes a few minutes!

### Q: Can I use this on my phone or tablet?
**A:** Yes! It works on any device with a web browser.

### Q: Will the contracts look professional?
**A:** Yes! They look exactly like your original PDF contracts - same formatting, same legal text, just with your specific information filled in.
